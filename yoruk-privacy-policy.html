Privacy Policy for Yoruk App

Thank you for using Yoruk, a GPS-based path tracking and direction-finding application. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the app collects, uses, and protects your data.

1. No Personal Data Collection

Yoruk does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The application processes only your device's location data locally and temporarily for the purpose of displaying information on your screen.

2. Use of Location Data

Yoruk uses GPS signals to determine your location.

It does not use compass sensors. Instead, direction is calculated using your recent GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude).

When the device is stationary, Yoruk does not provide real-time directional updates.

Location data is not recorded, not stored, and not transmitted anywhere.

3. No Internet Usage

The application operates entirely offline.

It does not access the internet and therefore does not send or receive any data over a network.

Due to the lack of internet usage, map services are not available.

Yoruk is designed to work independently of the deviceâ€™s compass and internet, making it especially useful in outdoor or emergency scenarios where:

  • Network signal is unavailable,
  • Compass readings are unreliable,
  • Or during sea travel or remote wilderness navigation.

5. Background Operation

Yoruk may continue to run in the background to provide continuous GPS-based tracking, but no data is retained or transmitted.
