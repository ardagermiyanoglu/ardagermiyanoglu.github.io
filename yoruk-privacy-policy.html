Privacy Policy for Yoruk App

Thank you for using Yoruk, a GPS-based path tracking and direction-finding application. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the app collects, uses, and protects your data.

1. No Personal Data Collection

Yoruk does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The application processes your device's location data locally and temporarily for the purpose of displaying information on your screen.

2. Use of Location Data

Yoruk uses GPS signals to determine your location and direction of movement.

All location data is processed on your device only. It is not recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

3. No Internet Usage

The application operates entirely offline.

It does not access the internet and therefore does not send or receive any data over a network.

Due to the lack of internet usage, map services are not available.

4. Background Operation

Yoruk may continue to run in the background to provide continuous GPS-based tracking, but no data is retained or transmitted.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed. Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated effective date.

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at: yoruk.support@protonmail.com
