Navigate with Offline Maps & GPS

Yoruk tracks your path using only your device's GPS and compass sensors. Whether you're in a forest, deep in the mountains, on the sea, or in an emergency zone, it helps you return to where you started — all without internet or mobile signal.

The latest version introduces offline maps, allowing you to view your location and route on a detailed base map — completely offline. Combined with visual route tracking, this gives you greater context and orientation in unfamiliar terrain.

Features

  • Fully Offline: Works without internet or network. GPS satellite coverage only.
  • Offline Maps: Includes built-in offline map support for better terrain awareness.
  • Compass Integration: Uses your device’s compass sensor when stationary, and GPS direction when moving.
  • Accurate Path Tracking: Your route is continuously recorded and shown on screen.
  • Distance to Start: Shows your distance from your starting point.
  • Scale & Accuracy: Distance scale adjusts with zoom level. GPS accuracy values are shown on screen.
  • Battery-Efficient: Simple, distraction-free interface optimized for long usage.
  • Private by Design: No data is collected, saved, or transmitted. Everything stays on your device.
