Find your way back — even without signal. Lightweight, privacy-first offline GPS tracker for outdoor, emergency, and tactical use.

"Yörük" is a Turkish word for a nomad — someone who roams nature without settling, just like this app helps you move freely and safely in offline environments.

Navigate Back Without Internet or Maps

Yoruk tracks your path using only your device's GPS and compass sensors. Whether you're in a forest, deep in the mountains, on the sea, or in an emergency zone, it helps you return to where you started — all without internet, mobile signal, or maps.

As you walk, your path is recorded and displayed visually. Using this path, you can retrace your steps even in the most remote areas. The app does not display maps; this is a deliberate design choice to ensure full independence from internet or signal availability.

Features

  • Fully Offline: Works without internet or network. GPS satellite coverage only.
  • Compass Integration: Uses your device’s compass sensor when stationary, and GPS direction when moving.
  • Accurate Path Tracking: Your route is continuously recorded and shown on screen.
  • Distance to Start: Shows your distance from your starting point.
  • Scale & Accuracy: Distance scale adjusts with zoom level. GPS accuracy values are shown on screen.
  • Battery-Efficient: Simple, distraction-free interface optimized for long usage.
  • Private by Design: No data is collected, saved, or transmitted. Everything stays on your device.

Use Case Scenarios

Even in complete signal blackouts, you can determine your direction based on your recorded route and position history. Ideal for:

  • Natural disasters / war zones / emergency evacuations
  • GPS-only environments: mountains, forests, deserts, sea
  • Individuals who value digital privacy and self-reliance

What Makes Yoruk Different?

  • Zero dependency on internet or mobile networks
  • No hidden data collection — true digital privacy
  • Compact and fast — always ready as an EDC tool
  • No maps means no distractions and no digital trace

Yoruk is your lightweight, reliable, privacy-first GPS navigation assistant when everything else fails.
